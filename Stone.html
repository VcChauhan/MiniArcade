Stone paper scissors( Rock paper scissors ) is a hand game usually played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand.

Stone

Shape of "rock" is a closed fist

Paper

Shape of "paper" is a flat hand

Scissor

Shape of "scissor" is a fist with the index finger and middle finger extended, forming a V
